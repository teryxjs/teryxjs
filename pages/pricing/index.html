Frequently asked questions

Is the Community edition really free?

Yes. Teryx Community is MIT licensed — free for personal and commercial use, forever. No time limits, no feature gates, no strings attached.

What are premium widgets?

Premium widgets include advanced grid features (pivot mode, Excel-like editing, master-detail rows) and enterprise chart types (heatmaps, treemaps, funnel charts) that go beyond the standard set.

Do I need Pro to use Teryx commercially?

No. The Community edition is MIT licensed and can be used in any commercial project. Pro is for teams that want priority support, premium widgets, or a commercial redistribution license.

When will Pro be available?

Teryx Pro is in development. Join the waitlist or follow us on GitHub to be notified when it launches. Community users will always be supported.
